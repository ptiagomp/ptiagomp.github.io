WHOIS Tiago?


Tiago Martins Pinto (1985 - Porto, Portugal) is a swiss-army knife media artist and academic, within the realm of socially engaged art practices. Currently a doctoral candidate in Contemporary Art at Aalto University in Finland, his academic journey includes a BA in Multimedia from IPB (Portugal) and an MA in Contemporary Art Creation from University of Aveiro (Portugal), where his focus was on audiovisual production and informal pedagogy within community-based arts.
Tiago's work has been supported by working and project grants from institutions such as the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation, Robert Bosch Foundation, Kone Foundation, and EMMA - Espoo Museum of Modern Art. His work has been showcased in Portugal, Germany, Canada, Iceland, and Finland.
