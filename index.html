WHOIS Tiago?


Tiago Martins Pinto (1985, Portugal) is a new media artist and art-based researcher revolving around community-engaged art and informal pedagogy practices. He is currently a doctoral candidate in Contemporary Art at Aalto University in Finland.
His artistic practice spans various disciplines, including video, installation, live-performance art, and scenography, having displayed his work in countries such as Portugal, Germany, Canada, Iceland, Finland and garnered support from several prestigious institutions, including the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation, Robert Bosch Foundation, Kone Foundation, and EMMA - Espoo Museum of Modern Art.
The smell of the ocean in the early morning, the mist and the wet sand are one of his fondest memories.
